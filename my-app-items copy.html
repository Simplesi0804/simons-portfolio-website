Stock Control

Production Tracker

The production application offers a list of products and associated materials for the production items. Users can declare production, track quantities, and view a progress bar. The app also includes quality checks and allows users to attach pictures for each declaration. It streamlines production management, ensuring efficient tracking of materials, quantities, progress, and quality.

Material Delivery Tracker

The material delivery tracker app efficiently manages material deliveries by tracking codes, quantities, and delivery dates. It sends email alerts for delayed deliveries and allows association with other computer systems. This streamlined app ensures timely and reliable material management.

Evening Production Report

Project evening report app enables quick reporting of the production and the quantity produced based on personnel. Users can declare if the production quantity is achieved, and the app automatically sends an email with this information. It enhances efficiency in project reporting and communication.

Finance Data

Finance Tracker

The finance tracker app efficiently tracks daily production quantities, costs and personnel. It calculates production costs, material costs, and provides an outcome of profit or loss. It simplifies financial monitoring and analysis.

Year vs Year Review

The year vs year review app compares production efficiency, tracks hazards in an area year over year, monitors incident reports, and compares the number of items produced annually. It provides valuable insights for analysis and decision-making.

Health and Safety

Building Access Control

The building access control app manages site access by tracking access cards assigned to personnel and notifies the Facilities manager of lost cards or unreturned cards. It ensures security and compliance by controlling site access and maintaining an audit trail of unused cards.

Training App

The training app offers on-demand information and guidance for managing the production area. It provides instructions on computer system transactions, auditing start codes, and filling in daily data Sheets. It empowers users with the knowledge and tools for effective production management.

Machine Pre-Use Checks

The machine pre-use check app ensures daily health and safety inspections for machinery in the production area. It alerts the health and safety manager via email if a machine is unfit for use, facilitating prompt resolution. The app serves auditing purposes and contributes to incident investigations.

Employee Engagement

Staff forum

The staff forum app enables staff members to log questions, queries, and suggestions. It includes kanban functionality for tracking progress and a "You Said We Did" section for updates. The app also provides access to forum meeting minutes and offers secure, private conversations with nominated forum members. It enhances communication and collaboration within the organization.

Personnel Request and Leave Log

The Personnel Request and Leave Log is a centralized system for managing Personnel. It facilitates requesting Personnel availability for upcoming weeks' production and tracks their shift patterns. The log also logs Personnel on Leave, allowing supervisors to plan effectively. By using this log, the Personnel App ensures efficient workforce management and seamless operations.

New Personnel Register

The New Personnel Register is a centralized record of production Personnel and their associated agencies. It keeps track of current Personnel members and maintains a log of previous personnel for potential re-requesting. This register streamlines workforce management, enhances communication, and optimizes staffing processes.|
