P1 - The Earth

As i kick started my journey into web design, I began with a captivating project focused on creating a simple information web page about our planet, Earth. Armed with newfound HTML skills gained via The Complete Web Developer Course 3.0 , I embarked on constructing a visually engaging webpage that featured a stunning image of Earth as its centerpiece. To enhance the immersive experience, I seamlessly embedded a captivating video showcasing Earth's mesmerizing rotation. This initial project served as a fantastic introduction to the world of web design, allowing me to combine creativity and technical proficiency to craft an interactive and informative digital space dedicated to our extraordinary planet.

P3 - Weather Scraper

During my web development journey via The Complete Web Developer Course 3.0 , I took on a project that involved creating a weather scraper using www.weather-forecast.com and PHP. I utilized web scraping techniques to extract a 3 day real-time weather data, such as temperature and wind speed, and presented it in a user-friendly format. This project deepened my understanding of PHP and how websites store and present information, while also nurturing my passion for coding and meteorology.
